Facebook Pixel: What is it?

Facebook Pixel is a code that you add to your website to monitor what happens to visitors who click on your Facebook advertisements. It assists you in gauging the success of your advertising campaigns by keeping an eye on important data like sales, conversions, and user behavior on your website. This data allows businesses to optimize their ad targeting, ensuring their adverts are delivered to the correct audience at the right time.

You may improve your campaigns and increase your return on investment (ROI) by using Facebook Pixel to better understand how users engage with your website.
